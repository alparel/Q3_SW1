Yahari Ore no Seishun Love Comedy wa Machigatteiru/Oregairu

Synopsis

Hachiman Hikigaya is an apathetic and narcissistic high school student who lacks motivation. He believes that carefree youth is nothing but a charade and that anyone who claims otherwise is only deluding themselves. Hachiman's teacher makes him join the Volunteer Service Club as a punishment for writing an essay ridiculing modern social connections; the club's mission is to lend a helping hand to any student who requests it in order to pursue their objectives. Hachiman finds himself dealing with other people's problems on the front lines, which is a position he never imagined he would be in since the only other club member is the stunning ice queen Yukino Yukinoshita. As Hachiman and Yukino use their wits to address the issues of several students, Will Hachiman's skewed perspective on society end up being a hindrance or a tool he can utilize to his advantage?


Main Characters


Hachiman Hikigaya

Hachiman Hikigaya is the main character of Yahari Ore no Seishun Love Come wa Machigatteiru and the point-of-view character of the anime. He is the second person to join the Service Club and a student at Sōbu High School's Class 2F.


Yukino Yukinoshita

Yukino Yukinoshita is the main heroine of Yahari Ore no Seishun Love Come wa Machigatteiru. She is a member of Sōbu High School's Class 2J and the Service Club's first president. Before Hachiman Hikigaya joined her, she was the only memeber in the club.


Yui Yuigahama

Class 2F student of Sōbu High School, Yui Yuigahama is the first recognized client of the Service Club. She later joins the Service Club as its third member.


Supporting Characters


Haruno Yukinoshita

The elder sister of Yukino Yukinoshita is Haruno Yukinoshita.


Saika Totsuka

Saika Totsuka is a student in Sōbu High School's Class 2F and serves as the school's tennis club president.


Hayato Hayama

Hayato Hayama, the captain and star of the Sōbu High School soccer team, is a prominent student of class 2-F. He is the most popular student in the school, and practically all of the girls have a crush on him.
